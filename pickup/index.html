Crescent Academy Pick Up Link

Instead of scanning QR codes and submitting forms in the queue, use the steps below to create a permanent 1-click link that you can save and reuse.
Step 1: Enable QR Scanner
Step 2: Take a screenshot of the PDF with the QR code and upload the image.
Step 3: Copy the link below and save it to your notes app. During pick up, just click on the link. It will automatically open and submit.
Are you storing any data? No. There is no backend processing or saving of images. This is all done on the front end. View the source here.
How does it work? It takes the string encoded in the QR code and replaces the action to view the form with an action to submit the form.
